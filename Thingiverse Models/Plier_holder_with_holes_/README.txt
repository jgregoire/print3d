# Summary

Tool holder for small pliers with holes for screwdrivers ect. 

Pockets were inspired from Parts Tray Drawers by FatalError3141
http://www.thingiverse.com/thing:398548
However they are a different size to better fit my pliers, so the drawers won't fit.

11 - 3.25" x .5" x 1.5"D Pockets
18 - .3" Holes
10 - .55" Holes

Prints fine without support
Took about 9hrs at 45mm/s

I printed with Cura
.5mm Nozzle
.3mm Layers 
1.1mm Shell
1mm Top and bottom
0% Infill
No support
